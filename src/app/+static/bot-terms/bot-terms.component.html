Before using our software make sure you fully understand how it works as you bear all the risks when using it with your funds

  • Bot does not use any external sources of exchange rates, it defines relative price of two assets as ratio between it's balances. For example bot has balances A and B, price will be A/B
  • Bot trades just few percents of it's total balance to make sure balances don't change too much after it's offer has been taken
  • When you commit your funds you deposit both assets traded by bot in equal value. If price of asset A grows relative to asset B, then when you withdraw you will get less of asset A and more of asset B. For example, if you committed 0.01 BTC and 100 USD and Bitcoin price goes from $10k to $100k, you will get approximately 10 times less BTC than you deposited.
